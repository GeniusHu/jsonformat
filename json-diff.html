Frequently Asked Questions

How do I compare two JSON files?

Paste your two JSON files into the left and right editors, or use the file upload buttons. The differences will be automatically highlighted, showing additions in green, deletions in red, and modifications in yellow.

Can I see a visual representation of JSON differences?

Yes, our tool provides a side-by-side view with color-coded highlighting to easily spot differences. You can also switch between different visualization modes including inline and structural views.

How do I export the comparison results?

You can export the comparison results in various formats including a unified diff format, HTML report, or copy the highlighted differences to your clipboard.
